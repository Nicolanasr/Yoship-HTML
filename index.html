01 Super Easy!

02 Start a Yo!

if you are Traveling international, or domestic whether by car, plane, or bike click on TRAVELLER. If you want your parcel to be picked up and shipped to you click on SHIPPER

03 YoTRAVELLER!

Add your travel iteneraries: location From - to, date of travel and arrival time. automatching with a shipper & negotiate offer

04 YoShipper!

Add your parcel, parcel location, pick up From - to and how much is your Payment offer and get Yo track Number and await automatching between users & negotiate offer

05 Get Notified!

TGet notified bout your shipment status i.e location, dates, offer, counter offers...

06 Communication!

Once offer agreed between Traveller & Shipper they can coordinate via email, calling, or texting. peace of mind from traveler with security deposit & scan barcode for shipper to secure delivery. Our platform is secure for communication and ensuring delivery

07 Delivery in 24 Hrs!

Delivery & payment within 24 hours traveler gets paid within 48 hours of delivery & deposit released. 12% commission for yoship
